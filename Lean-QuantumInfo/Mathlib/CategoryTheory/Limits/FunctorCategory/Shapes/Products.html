    (Co)products in functor categories #

    Given f : α → D ⥤ C, we prove the isomorphisms (∏ᶜ f).obj d ≅ ∏ᶜ (fun s => (f s).obj d) and (∐ f).obj d ≅ ∐ (fun s => (f s).obj d).
