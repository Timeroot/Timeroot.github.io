Quantum

Clifford Gate Puzzles

Global Phase: 1
How to Play
  • Click × between gates to merge them
  • Click 🔁 between H and Pauli to swap (commute) — the Pauli changes color!
  • Same gates annihilate; different Paulis → third color
  • Two half-gates (S) of same color → full gate
  • H×H = I (Hadamards self-annihilate)
  • Click a gate to split into two different Paulis
  • Shift+click to split reversed order
  • Ctrl+click to split into two half-gates (S×S)
